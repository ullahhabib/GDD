Zombie Clash is a 16-bit style 2D side-scroller game with the objective of successfully escaping from a zombie apocalypse to a safe island in a boat. The character would navigate a maze infested with enemy zombies. The player can kill them by jumping over them or by using a sword. While navigating, the player can obtain various power-ups and health items. Movement options include run, duck, jump, and moving in either of the four directions.
